About Me

From my earliest days working with relational databases, I've been fascinated by the power of data to drive decisions. With more than 11 years' experience in data engineering and architecture, I currently serve as a Data Architect at the Bank of England, where I design and maintain the platforms that underpin vital economic insights.

I studied Computer Engineering at Savitribai Phule Pune University and have continually expanded my expertise through certifications in Azure, AWS, Spark and Python. Beyond my day job, I'm passionate about open source and contribute to projects that solve real‑world data problems. This site is my space to share knowledge and connect with fellow data professionals.
